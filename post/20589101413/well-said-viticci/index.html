Well Said, Viticci

…of all the people I have talked to in the past two years about the iPad no one ever asked me about a smaller version.

I was surprised when Siegler wrote that people were saying they wished there was a smaller version of the iPad. I hang around with a lot of nerds, and even they haven’t really been “wishing” for a smaller iPad until the rumour mill started cranking recently.
